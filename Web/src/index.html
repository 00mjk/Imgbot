Why should I optimize my images?

When you’re shipping code and hitting deadlines, it’s easy to forget about optimizing your images. But the truth is if you don’t optimize your images, you are losing money.

Web pages with optimized images load faster. Faster pages have higher conversion rates, lower bounce rates, and happier users. And of course, smaller images reduce bandwidth costs for you and your visitors.

Always improving

When your site is fast then your users are happier, and when your users are happy, everyone is happy. This goes for apps and websites alike, if you want to load quickly, your images need to be optimized.
